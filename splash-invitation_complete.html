About Woop.ie

Woopie -Write Only Once, Publish It Everywhere, is a digital magazine publishing platform based on HTML5 & CSS3.

What we do

We want to help people create publications that look beautiful on every device, and the ability to export to many platforms including but not limited to ePub, PDF, web, & print.
