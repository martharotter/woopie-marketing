Features

Look what Woopie can do for your documents

↓

Multiple columns

Explore editorial layout possibilities with multiple columns, they stay responsive so your text looks great no matter what.

Videos

Add video files with youtube or vimeo, or upload your own to make your publication come alive!

Slideshows

Got beautiful photos? Show them off in a slidesow with optional captions and credits.

Pull quotes

Pull quotes are a great way to add style and add emphasis to certain phrases.

Multiple languages & fonts

Foreign character sets, right-to-left-text options and beautiful global fonts sourced from Google means it's easy to translate and globalize your publications.

Audio clips

If audio is part of your content, have no fear! Upload mp3 or ogg files or stream from sites like soundcloud to add automatic or on-click sounds.

Images that shine

Make your images come alive with full-width images, or images that pull left or right. Add optional credits, captions and alt text.

Import existing content

Woopie importers make it easy to reuse your content, whether it's on your blog, in iBooks format, or a PDF. Or start from scratch with Woopie templates!

Quizzes and Flip Cards

Add character and interactivity to your content with quizzes and flip cards. These are even usable in epub documents!

Call-out boxes

A call-out box (with optional images) is a way to set apart a section of text, either for some extra context, definitions or as an aside.

Custom themes

Use Woopie's beautiful and responsive themes, or use your own colors, fonts and logos in a custom theme that has your look-and-feel.

Always accessible publications

Woopie publications are accessible by default so they can be read and enjoyed by readers with different abilities.
