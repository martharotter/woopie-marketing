This Ghillie's Life

What is life like for those who earn a living full-time from ghillieing in Ireland? And is the way of the ghillie in danger of dying out?

By Daire Whelan | May 2014

We’ve talked a lot so far about how to decide on and pursue the career of your dreams. But once that offer’s in hand you’ll have a whole new challenge to face: negotiating pay. For some, this is a natural and easy part of the process. For others, it’s terrifying. It can feel counterintuitive to work so hard to get an offer and then potentially lose it due to pay negotiations.
