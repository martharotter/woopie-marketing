Le Cool Guides

COITE

Le Cool talks to Tomaso Anfossi and Francesco Ferrari of fashion label CO|TE...

We met about four years ago, while we were both collaborating with another brand. Initially it was just a friendship, but after a few years we decided to create something of our own that could really represent us. When we saw that by working together we were fascinated and amused at the same time we decided to create CO|TE. We both bring all our determination and will to pursue our project; being each other’s right-hand man is really important to us. The Made In Italy’s (see opposite) quality tradition is the very foundation of each of our creations. Even if we are young, we wish to carry on this tradition that allows Italian fashion to stand out all around the world. Italians have always been attracted to beauty, we are curious and we love to experiment. We also have a long history of quality and tailoring, which teaches us to respect the true value and quality that comes from the expertise of our artisans.

CO|TE is distinguished by its dynamic, young and fresh spirit: it’s a brand that is conceived for a naturally elegant Italy When you think ‘quality craftsmanship’, you automatically think ‘Italy’. It is only right, woman, who knows at the same time how to play with shapes, shades, colours and with a bright touch of irony.

We always try to frequent stimulating environments, which allows us to increase our knowledge and to enrich our minds, in order to transmit everything we see and learn into our creations. The inspiration comes from everything that surrounds us - a piece of fabric, an old garment found in a vintage shop, a snapshot, an old film or a geometric shape. Inspiration is everywhere, but as we are both very curious we are constantly looking for new ideas.

We love rich fabrics, which help to obtain and maintain the geometries that are so typical of CO|TE even when a garment is worn. We are always very attentive to the fabric’s quality and versatility. We always imagine a curious woman, who is daring and loves to have fun with our pieces. Fashion should be a friend of women and it should help them in every different situation they face.
