It’s no coincidence that every person I know who went from working for clients to working for themselves is happier.

Jim Coudal

Moving away from client services to building your own products is a desire many of us possess, but ultimately never realise. Jim Coudal has achieved it and with considerable style. With his talented team in Chicago he’s brought us JewelBoxing, The Deck Network, and everyone’s favourite made-in-the-USA notebook brand Field Notes. We found out how it all came about.

We spoke to Jim via Skype on 27th March 2012.
