They really loved the tape and they wanted to put it out as a twelve-inch single. I thought, ‘This is it. I don’t need to work in a factory, I’m going to make millions!’ I made £400.

Brendan Dawes

There are few people who could hold an audience for thirty minutes talking about their favourite pencil, but Brendan Dawes is one of them. From the factory floor to presenting with Hillman Curtis, his journey is truly inspiring. We caught up with Brendan at the end of 2011, just before he made the decision to switch paths once again and close the doors to his product company Beep Industries.

We spoke to Brendan at the Millennium Gloucester hotel, London, on 25th November 2011.
