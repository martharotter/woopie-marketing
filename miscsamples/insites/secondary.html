Love What You Do

by Aarron Walter
from MailChimp

For most people, the pursuit of happiness is something best saved for the weekend. Passion is for hobbies, not for jobs.

But we know that work is best when it’s instilled with love and care. As you read this book, you’ll discover that an ocean of passion spills forth from the work of each contributor, unconstrained by the containers of life, and energising all in its path. Count the projects they pursue and the people they influence, and you’ll be humbled. Their prodigious careers make us all feel like slackers.

It’s not pedigree that’s the secret to our contributors’ success. The web is a meritocracy, where passion and persistence are what place us in positions of influence. As you marvel at the insights and intelligence of Jeffrey Zeldman, Mandy Brown, Trent Walton, Tina Roth Eisenberg and their fellow authors, you might find yourself thinking, ‘how can I reach that level of excellence?’

Though their backgrounds are diverse, there’s one trait our contributors all share: they love what they do. From that love springs their trademark craftsmanship and relentless curiosity. Their passion is what we appreciate about their work, and in turn, it’s what we appreciate about them.

If you’re encouraged by the insights in this book, get in touch with what you love. That’s always the best place to start.
