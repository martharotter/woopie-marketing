How to make a new publication:

Our publications work like this - you have a publication, which could be a book, a magazine, a report, an article, or something else entirely.

Publications like magazines, where publishing happens every month or so, are made up of issues. Issues are made up of individual articles.

Other publications, like books, might have individual sections or chapters. You can control how your publication is structured, so don't about this now - you can always change it later!

Create a New Publication

How to create a publication

A publication is what we call your title - it can be a regular publication that comes out every month, or just a one-off publication. You publication is your title, for example The Monthly Movie Review or The Weekly Company Newsletter.

To create your publication, select New Publication from the dashboard (just after you log in) and select one of the icons to get started.

Some of the things you might want to customize about your publication:

  1. A title - e.g. Cupcakes Monthly
  2. A tagline - e.g. All the latest Cupcakes news
  3. A logo or thumbnail to help you identify your publication on the listings page
  4. A visual theme. (the theme defines the colors and typography used in your publication - more on that later)

Once you've created your publication, you can click Edit Publication in the Articles listing page to update the theme or add extra details such as your Twitter, Google Analytics and Facebook IDs for social sharing and tracking.

Got Issues?

If you are creating issues for your publication, you will see them here

Not all publications need issues. Some, like books or reports, just have individual chapters or sections.

But if you publish your content on a regular basis, say weekly or monthly, issues are a good way to organize them.

You can customize your issue to change the title, volume, issue number, slug, and imagery for the issue.
