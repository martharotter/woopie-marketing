ReadyForZero

Day 1: Introduction

Welcome to the F3 Challenge! This ebook is all about helping you create and maintain positive changes in your life. Whether your main focus is on food, fitness, or finance (or a combination of all three), we’ll be sharing tips, resources, and motivation to help you take aim at your goal and begin your journey.

Why Food, Fitness and Finance?

Year after year, goals relating to health, exercise, and finance rank highly among the most common New Year’s Resolutions. The persistence of these goals suggests that it’s not just an individual struggle, but something that challenges all of us. Unfortunately, however admirable and worthwhile these resolutions may be, they aren’t often met with long-term success.

The truth is, most worthy goals are created with good intentions but are often forgotten or abandoned once we lose our motivation or face unpredictable struggles.

To avoid resolution slump, we’re focusing on the method of making a positive change rather than just calling for a resolution to make a change. With the Food, Fitness, and Finance Challenge, we’re going to work together to show you how positive actions can be used to overcome challenges in all areas of your life. By learning ways to successfully create and maintain a healthy habit, you’re building the structure for a lifestyle change. It’s like creating a template that you can apply wherever you need it.

At the core, success in finance, food, or fitness is achieved by:

  • creating a long-term plan
  • making healthy choices
  • placing value on your long term and short term livelihood
  • checking in with yourself regularly and maintaining balance to achieve satisfaction

This is the template you can use to make changes in all three areas. Striving to achieve in just one area, much less three simultaneously, is a big challenge. But behavioral tactics you undertake to make positive changes in one area can be used to achieve your goals in other areas. Our goal is to show you how.

So what exactly is a positive change? The answer is, of course, specific to the individual. But we can agree that a positive change is one that nurtures your goals and adds energy to your life rather than adding stress and pain. As you read each chapter, you can take the opportunity to consider what that means in your life. For some, it might be adding something - for others, it might be taking something away. Either way, focus on your goals and keep refining them over the next two weeks.

Since this idea originated from a two week challenge, each chapter of this ebook will represent one day. But we want to give you the platform to achieve your personal positive changes - not just for the next 14 days of your life, but for each new day as it comes. Months, years, tens of years and beyond.

Because big changes start with small steps, we’re going to take it one day at a time.
