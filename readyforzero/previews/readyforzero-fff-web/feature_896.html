Day 14: Conclusion

Though it’s only been 14 days, we’ve covered a huge amount of ground and we want to thank you for sticking with us the whole way. We created this resource to show you that the tactics you use to overcome one area of your life can be utilized in other areas of your life as well. We hope you will continue to use the skills and tools we’ve provided to motivate you towards making a positive and sustainable lifestyle changes.

Let’s take a moment to recap how far we’ve come:

We started our challenge by talking about the importance of mindfulness in the choices that we make. When we’re present and aware of the impact of our past and present choices, we’re able to acknowledge that we have the power of choice. That sense of control guides us towards taking action and making positive changes in the future. To keep us focused on our goals, we also looked at the importance of creating a sustainable and realistic plan that anticipates and adjusts for the precariousness of life. And despite the obstacles and challenges that we may face, having a solid foundation and game plan is key in giving us the support that we need to achieve our goal.

But perhaps most importantly, we talked about the importance of celebrating wins… small and large. You’ve come far - take a minute to give yourself the recognition for your accomplishments! Congratulations on dedicating your time and energy to making a positive change.

Even though we may have come to the close of our challenge, that doesn’t mean your challenge needs to end here. Every day is the perfect time to make a positive change in your life. A healthy lifestyle will always be a work in progress. No matter how organized you are, no matter how strict you are...remember that life is dynamic. By compiling and utilizing the tools necessary to face challenges, you’ll be ready to face any curveball that’s thrown at you while continuing to move towards your goal. With this resource, we hope we’ve given you the support you need to continue and succeed in your goals!

Now it’s time to pass the reigns. You’re in charge of this journey and you have the tools to make the change. So stop and ask for directions, fuel up with inspiration and advice when you need to...but keep on going.

Congratulations and thank you for inspiring so many others!
