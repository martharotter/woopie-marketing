Level 3: How to Secure Your Financial Future

Okay, once you’ve made it to level three then you’re out of debt (or close) and you are ready to cement some more good financial habits to protect your future.

Here are some new habits you can learn:

1. How to Build a Savings Fund: This post will teach you strategies for building up your savings fund and give you insight into how much you should have in your savings in order to be prepared for unexpected emergencies.

2. How to Start Investing: This post will help you learn about the different types of investments and how to balance your portfolio in order to avoid too much risk.

3. How to Build a Retirement Fund: This post explains the way retirement funds work and how to use your own retirement fund to put yourself in a good position for your own eventual retirement.
