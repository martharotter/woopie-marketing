Crime Fiction Fix

Your technical questions answered

This is the section for you, writers. Have you written that one section in the book, but aren’t sure you’ve done it right? Does a mortuary look like that? How is an excavation dig set up to retrieve remains? Is that really the law in Mongolia?

As writers, our imaginations can often reach past the limits of our fact intelligence. That’s what we’re for! If you have a question, we will try to find the answer for you. We have contacts in professional fields from police chiefs in the US to forensic archaeologists who have done digs in places like Iran.

If you can imagine it, we will try our hardest to find you the most correct way to write it.

Every month (with prior permission from the writer) we will post some of your questions and answers here to share with our fellow crime-fiction writers. No writer should feel s/he is in this alone and we’re all here to support each other.

If you have a question, send an email to questions@crime-fiction-fix.com and we’ll get fact checking as soon as we can.

We are all one big community and want to see each other thrive in something we are passionate about.
