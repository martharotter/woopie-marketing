Crime Fiction Fix

Letter from the editor



 

Welcome to the very first issue of the new crime writing magazine, Crime Fiction Fix.

This is a magazine with three very distinct characteristics which set it apart from all the other superb crime-fiction magazines out there:

  1. Every month we will bring to your inbox a substantial video interview with an established crime-fiction writer, giving you an insight into their background, their thinking and their working methods.
  2. Each issue will contain insights into the actualities of dealing with crime on the street, in the laboratory and in the courtroom – insights which will be of interest to all, and invaluable to the writers amongst us.
  3. Every issue will showcase new writing – both from newly-published authors and from amongst our members.

Our aim is to be interesting, useful, informative and brief – a shot in the arm for the crime fiction aficionado and a mind-enhancing experience for the crime-fiction writer.

Above all, though, our aim is to provide what you want – so:

  • If you’d like more of some things and less of something else, please let us know.
  • If you’d like a regular feature on something that’s not here already, please let us know.
  • If you’ve got favorite items you’d like us to run, please let us know.

This is your crime-fiction writing magazine – your monthly Fix. Help us make sure that every month is the right mix for you.

Keep reading, keep writing, keep in touch.

Yours,


Sarah Williams
