The Council of Concern Worldwide presents its report and consolidated financial statements for the year ended December 31, 2014.

In October, we screened 22,230 children for malnutrition in Malawi.

EDUCATION 400

In Haiti, we taught 400 women in adult literacy to increase their earning potential. We also offered them training in running a small business.
