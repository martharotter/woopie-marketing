Health


77

We supported healthcare in 77 health facilities and six refugee settlements in Uganda.


2,017

We distributed 2,017 insecticide-treated bed nets in Chad, and taught communities how to use them to prevent malaria.


47

Last year none of the participants in our Burundi livelihoods programme washed their hands with soap after going to the toilet. Now, 47 per cent do so.

WE HELPED TO IMPROVE THE HEALTH OF 2.8 MILLION PEOPLE DIRECTLY IN 2014, AND 7.4 MILLION INDIRECTLY. 6.4 MILLION WERE FEMALE .

Concern believes that we all have a right to good nutrition and healthcare. Health and nutrition are even more important for young children, pregnant women, elderly people, and people displaced from their homes.

To achieve maximum impact, we work in the most vulnerable communities. On the ground, we work with local people and partners to identify the households most in need of help. We also work with local and national governments to strengthen healthcare systems and increase access to good healthcare and nutrition support.
