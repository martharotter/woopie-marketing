Report of Council

EMERGENCIES 1,756

LIVELIHOODS 83

We helped 1,756 displaced families in Lebanon prepare for winter by distributing items including stoves, blankets, mattresses, fuel vouchers and tent waterproofing kits.

83 per cent of families benefiting from our livelihoods programme in Rwanda now eat at least two meals a day, compared with 31.3 per cent in 2011.
